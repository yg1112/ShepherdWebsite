Privacy Policy

Shepherd by DZG STUDIO LLC

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Shepherd is a macOS menu bar utility that monitors application windows for specific keywords and activity. Your data stays on your Mac. This policy describes how Shepherd handles data locally and what information is transmitted if you choose to enable the optional Remote Supervision feature.

2. How Monitoring Works

All monitoring runs entirely on your Mac. Shepherd reads text content from application windows using native macOS Accessibility APIs and AppleScript. Keyword matching, alert evaluation, and all related logic execute locally on your device. No monitored content leaves your computer unless you explicitly enable Remote Supervision.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No audio capture or microphone access
  • No keystroke logging or keyboard input monitoring
  • No analytics SDKs, telemetry, or behavioral tracking
  • No user data used for training models or algorithms

4. Remote Supervision (Pro)

Shepherd Pro includes an optional Remote Supervision feature that forwards alerts to a remote endpoint so you can stay informed from anywhere. When enabled, only the following metadata is transmitted:

  • The name of the monitored window
  • The type of alert triggered
  • The matched keyword that caused the alert
  • A timestamp of when the alert occurred

The actual text content being monitored is never sent. Only alert metadata leaves your Mac.

5. Data Storage

Shepherd does not maintain a cloud database. Alert metadata sent through Remote Supervision is processed by a Cloudflare Worker and is ephemeral — it is not stored persistently. There is no user account system within Shepherd.

Local preferences and configuration are stored on your Mac using standard macOS mechanisms (UserDefaults and Keychain) and remain entirely under your control.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare Workers — Used to relay alert metadata for Remote Supervision. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to data processed in transit.
  • LemonSqueezy — Handles subscription payments for Shepherd Pro. Payment information is collected and processed entirely by LemonSqueezy; Shepherd does not receive or store your payment details.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@dzgapp.com.
